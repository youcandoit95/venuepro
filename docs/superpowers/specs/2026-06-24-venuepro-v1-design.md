## 1. Overview & Scope

VenuePro is a court-booking platform for Indonesia (Jakarta first), launching with **padel & tennis**, extensible to other venue types later. Players discover venues and book/pay; venues manage courts, schedule, pricing, bookings, and refunds. The **public discovery + booking surface is SSR for SEO**; the **venue dashboard + platform admin are Livewire PWAs**.

**Monetisation:** venues buy **prepaid credits**; each confirmed online booking deducts **1 credit** (configurable). Platform revenue = credit sales only. Player payment goes **directly to the venue** (venue = merchant of record via Xendit). The platform never touches booking money.

### v1 In-Scope
1. **Identity, roles & account lifecycle** ÔÇö phone/WhatsApp OTP auth; roles: player, venue_owner, venue_staff, platform_admin. **Self-service account deletion ("Hapus akun")** in account settings (in-app + web) and **published Privacy Policy + Terms** pages ÔÇö both are UU PDP obligations *and* hard Google Play / App Store submission requirements, so they ship in v1 to keep "release a mobile app fast" real.
2. **Venue & courts** ÔÇö CRUD, geo (PostGIS), amenities; **operating hours + date-specific overrides** (holidays); **peak/off-peak pricing**; **availability blocks** (maintenance/event/weather/private).
3. **Discovery (public, SSR + SEO)** ÔÇö search by sport/location(radius)/price/availability; venue + court pages; schema.org; **self-hosted map (facade)**.
4. **Availability & booking** ÔÇö slot generation (duration options + turnaround buffer + band-aware pricing); **DB-enforced no-overlap**; hold/expiry; staff **walk-in** entry.
5. **Payments (venue merchant-of-record)** ÔÇö player pays 100% to the **venue's Xendit (xenPlatform sub-account)**; **platform credit wallet** deducts per booking; webhook-driven, idempotent. **No platform fund custody.**
6. **Cancellation / refund** ÔÇö per-venue **CancellationPolicy**; **the venue performs refunds** (from its own Xendit balance) per its policy; **hard, prominent disclosures on every transaction page**; service fee non-refundable.
7. **Trust** ÔÇö **venue ratings/reviews** by players (accountability mechanism).
8. **Notifications** ÔÇö WhatsApp confirmation + reminder; email fallback (queued).
9. **Venue dashboard (Livewire PWA)** ÔÇö calendar, bookings, walk-in, pricing, blocks, cancellation policy, refunds, **credit balance + top-up**, reviews.
10. **Platform admin** ÔÇö venue onboarding/moderation, credit packages & top-ups, oversight.
11. **PWA / SEO / Lighthouse ÔëÑ99 shell** ÔÇö installable + **mobile-app-packaging-ready (TWA + iOS Capacitor)**, offline shell, **all assets & fonts self-hosted (no CDN)**, schema.org, sitemap, per-route Lighthouse CI ÔëÑ99.

### v1.5 (next spec, designed-for now)
- **VPR rating** (margin-aware Elo+RD; padel doubles, tennis singles+doubles) seeded by **"friendly match with confirmed score"** (breaks the ratingÔåömatchmaking circular dependency).
- **Open Match / matchmaking** (per-head payment, team balancing, state machine, anti-gaming).
- Automated payouts/disbursement is N/A here (venue holds funds), but credit auto-billing / metering refinements land here.

### Out of Scope (later)
Recurring bookings, waitlist auto-rebook, full dynamic/last-minute pricing, memberships/prepaid player credits, coaching scheduling, tournaments/season-series (feed VPR), multi-venue chain analytics, native apps, in-app chat.

## 2. Market & Operating Context (Jakarta 2026)

Padel supply has matured; operator pain = **utilisation (off-peak), no-shows, and discovery** vs the old "no courts" problem. Booking is still WhatsApp/IG DM + manual transfer + screenshot. Payments: **QRIS + e-wallet + VA** dominate; **WhatsApp** is comms; **Google Maps** is discovery. Operators currently demand manual transfer because **they control the money** ÔÇö so VenuePro keeps them as merchant of record (money goes straight to them), which is also the lowest-regulation path.

### 2.1 Competitive landscape & VenuePro's wedge

Two incumbents Indonesian players already use, occupying **different** halves of the problem ÔÇö VenuePro's wedge is the intersection neither owns: **trusted, instant, in-app booking where the venue keeps 100% of the money, with fair matchmaking and a fast SSR/PWA web that actually converts.**

- **Reclub** (community/matchmaking app; 500K+ global installs, padel-strong in Jakarta). Owns the **social/Open-Match** layer (round-robin, Americano/Mexicano, level-based games, clubs/chat) and has a **good SSR/SEO web** (indexed club/meet pages). **But it processes NO money** ÔÇö "hosts" collect via **manual bank transfer + payment-proof screenshots**, which has produced documented **fraud / fictitious-booking / no-refund** complaints; its **player rating is self-declared 1ÔÇô10 and openly sandbagged** (no objective rating for padel/tennis; DUPR is pickleball-only); its **web is read-only** (must install a 188 MB app to transact, freeze/blank-screen bugs reported); and it has **no venue-as-business tooling** (no court├ùtime inventory, no operator dashboard, no disbursement). Monetizes a thin player subscription (~$2ÔÇô3/mo), capturing **0% of GMV**.
- **AYO** (the real booking competitor; 2,010+ venues, in-app QRIS/e-wallet, disburses to venues). Owns **booking-with-payment** + "AYO Venue Management." *(Full weakness analysis pending the AYO research pass ÔÇö fold into this section when ready.)*

**How VenuePro beats Reclub (each tied to a real, sourced Reclub gap):**
1. **"Bayar ke venue, bukan ke orang asing."** Venue = merchant-of-record on its **own Xendit** ÔåÆ players pay a verified business; money is auditable + refundable, never "disappears with the host." Directly answers Reclub's #1 fraud/no-refund pain.
2. **Real in-app payment** (QRIS/e-wallet/card) vs screenshot-proof transfers ÔÇö and the venue keeps **100%** (beats AYO's commission *and* Reclub's no-payment).
3. **Court-level inventory + instant confirmed booking** (Court 3, 7ÔÇô8pm, book now) vs Reclub's "request ÔåÆ wait ÔåÆ maybe" host sessions; the **DB EXCLUDE constraint** kills double-/fictitious-booking.
4. **Margin-aware VPR rating** (v1.5) beats unverified self-rating ÔåÆ fair, hard-to-game matchmaking for padel + tennis. Lead the *"no more sandbaggers"* narrative.
5. **Prepaid credits** double as a clean **instant refund-to-wallet rail** + venue-friendly monetization (no commission); Reclub can't refund at all.
6. **A proper venue operator dashboard** (calendar, occupancy, revenue, walk-in/POS, peak pricing) ÔÇö Reclub has none.
7. **Web that transacts** (SSR + installable PWAÔåÆnative) ÔÇö capture the exact organic intent Reclub's read-only web leaks back to its app; out-SEO it *with* on-page booking.
8. **Performance + trustworthy notifications** (Lighthouse-99 PWA vs a freeze-prone 188 MB app; granular opt-in, quiet-hours notifications vs Reclub's named spam problem).
9. **System-enforced no-show/deposit protection + verified identities + a real dispute/refund flow** backed by the payment trail ÔÇö protecting venues *and* players where Reclub protects neither.

> **Positioning rule:** don't try to out-*community* Reclub on day one ÔÇö **out-*trust* and out-*transact*** it, and ship just enough Open-Match/level-based social (v1.5) to neutralize its stickiness.

## 3. Legal / Tax / Custody Position (load-bearing)

- **Platform never holds booking funds.** Using **Xendit xenPlatform managed sub-accounts**, each venue is a sub-account that **owns its funds**; charges are created "on behalf of" the venue ÔåÆ 100% lands in the venue's sub-account. The platform is **not a fund custodian** ÔåÆ avoids PJP/escrow licensing (BI 23/6/2021).
- **Platform is a SaaS vendor, not a marketplace payment-collector** ÔåÆ **not the PMK 37/2025 0.5% PPh collector** (that regime targets platforms that collect/route seller payments). Platform income = **credit sales** only.
- **Tax (platform's own income):** below the **PKP threshold (Rp4.8 B/yr turnover)** the platform is **non-PKP ÔåÆ no PPN, no e-faktur**, paying only **PPh final 0.5%** on gross (PP 55/2022). e-Faktur/PPN becomes required only upon PKP registration.
- **Venue is merchant of record** ÔåÆ handles its own booking-revenue taxes (its sale, its PPN/PPh). The platform stays out of the booking tax loop.
- **Disclaimer:** this is the structurally-minimal position, not formal tax/legal advice ÔÇö **confirm with a tax consultant + Xendit onboarding/KYB** before production.

## 4. Architecture

- **Laravel 12 ┬À PHP 8.4.** **Blade (SSR public) + Livewire 3 + Alpine + Tailwind** (dashboard/admin). Server-rendered, minimal JS ÔåÆ the lowest-risk path to **Lighthouse ÔëÑ98 + SEO + offline**.
- **PostgreSQL** + **PostGIS** (radius search) + full-text search. Extensions: `btree_gist` (no-overlap constraint).
- **Redis**: cache (hot availability), queues (notifications, webhooks side-effects), sessions, and a **fast-fail UX lock** for booking (not the integrity guarantee).
- **Payments: Xendit xenPlatform** ÔÇö managed sub-accounts (venue = sub-account, owns funds), charges (QRIS/e-wallet/VA), webhooks. Platform credit wallet billed separately.
- **Notifications:** WhatsApp Business API (provider TBD at impl) + email fallback; queued.
- **Multi-tenancy (corrected):** **do NOT global-scope public read models** (discovery queries across all venues). Tenant-scope **only the dashboard surface**, binding the active venue **from the route** (not an implicit global scope). Platform-admin access via `Gate::before`, not `withoutGlobalScopes`. `venue_id` denormalised onto bookings for cheap scoping + constraints. **Isolation tests required.**
- **Build:** Vite; **all CSS/JS/fonts/images self-hosted/local ÔÇö zero external CDN.**

## 5. Data Model (v1)

Single Postgres DB; PostGIS geometry; `venue_id` denormalised where needed.

**Identity:** `users` (name, phone[WA], email?, role) ┬À OTP via phone/WA.
**Venue/courts:** `venues` (owner_id, name, slug, description, address, geom, status[pending|active|suspended], amenities, rating_avg, rating_count, xendit_subaccount_id) ┬À `courts` (venue_id, name, sport[padel|tennis], surface, environment[indoor|outdoor], attributes, buffer_min, default_duration_min, active).
**Schedule/pricing:** `operating_hours` (court_id|venue_id, weekday, open, close) ┬À `operating_hour_overrides` (date-specific holiday hours/closures) ┬À `pricing_rules` (court_id, time-band, tier[peak|offpeak], price_idr, duration_min). ┬À `availability_exceptions` (court_id|venue_id, starts_at, ends_at, reason[maintenance|event|holiday|weather|private], recurring?).
**Booking:** `bookings` (venue_id, court_id, player_id?, **booked_range tstzrange GENERATED from starts_at+duration_min**, starts_at, duration_min, status[hold|paid|confirmed|cancelled|no_show], price_idr, service_fee_idr, source[online|walkin], hold_expires_at, payment_ref).
  - **Integrity:** `EXCLUDE USING gist (court_id WITH =, booked_range WITH &&) WHERE (status IN ('hold','paid','confirmed'))` ÔåÆ overlapping bookings are a hard DB error (handles 60/90/120-min crossings). Walk-ins go through the same constraint.
**Payments:** `payments` (booking_id, venue_id, provider[xendit], provider_ref, method[qris|ewallet|va], amount_idr, status[pending|paid|failed|refunded], settlement_status, raw_payload) ÔÇö **funds belong to the venue's sub-account; platform records but never holds.** ┬À `webhook_events` (provider, event_id **UNIQUE**, processed_at) for idempotency.
**Credits (platform monetisation):** `credit_wallets` (venue_id, balance) ┬À `credit_transactions` (venue_id, type[topup|booking_deduct|refund_reverse|adjustment], amount, booking_id?, balance_after) ┬À `credit_packages` (name, credits, price_idr) ÔÇö platform sells these (its only revenue).
**Cancellation/refund:** `cancellation_policies` (venue_id, tiers JSON[{hours_before, refund_pct}], grace_min, no_show_after_min, service_fee_refundable[false], notes) ┬À refunds executed by venue via Xendit; logged on `payments`.
**Trust:** `venue_reviews` (venue_id, player_id, booking_id, rating[1ÔÇô5], comment, created_at) ÔåÆ maintains `venues.rating_avg/count`.
**Ops:** `notifications_log`.

*(v1.5 tables ÔÇö reserved, not built in v1: `player_ratings`, `matches`/`match_sides`/`match_sets`/`match_confirmations`, `rating_history`, `open_matches`/`open_match_participants`.)*

## 6. Key Flows & Error Handling

### 6.1 Booking + payment (venue merchant-of-record, no double-booking)
1. Player selects court+date+slot; server checks `bookings` **and `availability_exceptions`** for overlap.
2. Acquire **Redis UX lock** (fast fail) + DB transaction; **INSERT booking(status=hold, booked_range, hold_expires_at=now()+10min)** ÔÇö the **EXCLUDE constraint** is the real guarantee; a `23P01` conflict ÔåÆ "slot just taken".
3. Create a Xendit charge **on the venue's sub-account** (QRIS/e-wallet/VA); **charge expiry = hold expiry**. Optional **service fee** (venue-configurable; covers the venue's MDR ÔÇö i.e. player can be made to bear processing cost) added to the charge.
4. **Webhook** (verify `x-callback-token`; insert into `webhook_events` first for idempotency): on `paid`, **atomic conditional transition** `UPDATE ÔÇª WHERE id=? AND status='hold' AND hold_expires_at>now()`; if 0 rows ÔåÆ late payment ÔåÆ trigger venue refund + notify. On success ÔåÆ `confirmed`, **deduct 1 credit** from the venue wallet (`credit_transactions`), enqueue WA confirmation + reminder.
5. **Insufficient credits:** if the venue wallet is empty, online bookings are **paused for that venue** until top-up (the monetisation lever); walk-ins still allowed.
6. **Timeouts/failures:** hold expires ÔåÆ job releases (DELETE/cancel hold) + cancel the pending charge.
7. **Walk-in:** staff create `booking(source=walkin, status=confirmed)` directly through the same EXCLUDE constraint (no online payment; **no credit deduction** ÔÇö inventory only).
- **Allowed-transition matrix** prevents stale/out-of-order webhooks from overwriting terminal states.

### 6.2 Cancellation / refund (venue-owned)
- Each transaction page shows a **hard, unmissable disclosure** before payment: the **venue's cancellation policy**, "**refunds are handled by the venue**", "**service fee is non-refundable**", "**venue-initiated cancellation (e.g. weather/maintenance) = full refund**".
- Player or venue initiates cancellation ÔåÆ policy computes refund %. **The venue executes the Xendit refund from its own balance** via the dashboard; platform logs it and **reverses the deducted credit** if cancelled before play. No-show auto-marked after `no_show_after_min` (staff-overridable).
- Accountability is via **venue rating** ÔÇö venues that mishandle refunds get downrated.

### 6.3 Credit top-up
Venue buys a `credit_package` ÔåÆ pays the **platform** (this IS platform revenue; the only money the platform receives) ÔåÆ `credit_transactions(topup)`. Low-balance reminders.

## 7. Non-Functional Requirements

> The owner's six hard requirements ÔÇö **SEO ranks ┬À PWA ships fast as a mobile app ┬À Lighthouse ÔëÑ99 every route ┬À secure from all common web attacks ┬À easy to use ┬À comfortable UI/UX (spacing/padding/font)** ÔÇö are specified below. The load-bearing architectural decision underpinning all of them is a **hard partition between two surfaces on one codebase**: the **public discovery/venue/booking pages are independent SSR multi-page documents** (indexable, fast, deep-linked *into*), and the **authenticated dashboard/admin is a persistent Livewire app-shell SPA** (installable, packageable as a native app). They share design tokens but are routed, cached, and packaged separately. Cross-lens tradeoffs are resolved in **Appendix A**.

### 7.1 Lighthouse ÔëÑ 99 ÔÇö per-route, all four categories, CI-gated
- **Per-route CI matrix (replaces "ÔëÑ98 on key pages").** `@lhci/cli` autorun, **mobile Moto-G-class preset** (4├ù CPU throttle, 1.6 Mbps, 150 ms RTT), `numberOfRuns: 3` median, GitHub Actions matrix (one URL per leg) against a **seeded staging build** (3ÔÇô8 anchor venues so discovery/map render real volume). Audit **every route class** ÔÇö `/`, `/{sport}/{city}`, `/venue/{slug}`, `/venue/{slug}/book`, plus a representative authed `/dashboard` route (seeded session cookie) ÔÇö asserting **performance, accessibility, best-practices, AND seo each ÔëÑ 0.99**. Audit the dashboard in **three states**: cold load, after ÔëÑ5 scripted `wire:navigate` hops (steady-state SPA), and SW-warmed. Any route/state < 0.99 fails the pipeline.
- **Per-template asset budgets (`budgets.json` + `size-limit`, CI-enforced).** Public SSR routes Ôëñ **30 KB JS (br)**, Ôëñ **14 KB critical CSS inline** (design-system target Ôëñ 10 KB) + deferred remainder, LCP image Ôëñ **90 KB AVIF**, **one** font file Ôëñ **40 KB**. Dashboard Ôëñ **80 KB JS** (Livewire+Alpine core) + per-component HTML payload Ôëñ 40 KB. Every route: **TBT < 150 ms, CLS = 0, LCP < 2.5 s** throttled. Third-party requests = **0**. Vite `manualChunks` so Alpine/Livewire **never load on public SSR routes** ÔÇö CI fails if a public entrypoint imports the Livewire runtime.
- **LCP discipline.** Venue imagery as responsive **AVIF (WebP fallback)** at 320/640/960/1280 via `<picture>`/`srcset`; **every `<img>` carries explicit width/height** (store `width,height` columns on `venue_images` so SSR emits dimensions ÔåÆ CLS = 0). LCP element is a **preloaded image** (`fetchpriority=high`, `<link rel=preload as=image>`), not text, keeping font-swap off the LCP path. Server-side image pipeline (Intervention / Spatie Media Library) transcodes on upload, strips EXIF, AVIF qÔëê50ÔÇô60.
- **Map = static AVIF facade** with a play-button overlay; MapLibre GL + self-hosted Jakarta PMTiles dynamically imported **only on click**. CI asserts **zero map JS on initial load**.
- **Zero render-blocking + font-swap CLS = 0.** Inline per-template critical CSS, defer the rest; **one Plus Jakarta Sans variable woff2** (Latin + Latin-Ext, axis 400ÔÇô700, Ôëñ 40 KB), preloaded `crossorigin`; `font-display: optional` on the LCP heading (swap elsewhere); **metric-matched `@font-face` fallback** (`size-adjust` / `ascent-override` / `descent-override` / `line-gap-override` measured against `local()` Roboto/SF) so swap contributes **0.000** to CLS.
- **CSP at zero perf cost.** **No inline scripts** ÔÇö consolidate SW-register / map-facade / install-prompt into one hashed deferred external JS (< 5 KB) so public `script-src` needs neither `unsafe-inline` nor a per-request nonce; `@alpinejs/csp` build (no `unsafe-eval`). Public CSP is **hash-based and static**, set at the edge (so it never blocks full-page caching or raises TTFB); per-request nonces **only** on the dynamic dashboard. Self-hosting stays the **default**, but CSP is an **allowlist, not a wall** ÔÇö any asset/service that genuinely can't be self-hosted is permitted by adding its specific origin to the relevant directive (see ┬º7.4); such externals load off the public LCP/critical path so the ÔëÑ99 budget holds.
- **App-shell without main-thread creep.** `@persist`/`wire:persist` **only** the top bar + bottom nav; `wire:navigate` fully tears down/rebuilds the content component each nav (never `@persist` content) so Alpine instances/listeners GC and TBT/INP/memory stay flat across a session ÔÇö proven by the steady-state + SW-warmed audits.
- **Livewire partial-render CLS.** Booking calendar reserves fixed box dimensions for slot grid, price, and hold-timer (`tabular-nums`); SSR the initial day's slot grid (FCP/LCP don't wait on a round-trip); scope the Livewire component to the slot-grid partial; `wire:navigate.hover` prefetch on venueÔåÆbook.
- **TTFB/LCP floor.** Optional **Laravel Octane + FrankenPHP** worker mode (audit singletons for cross-request leaks, bound memory, load-test first); Redis hot-availability cache; short-TTL fragment cache for SSR discovery/venue keyed by sport+city+filters; brotli + immutable long-cache on hashed assets; HTTP/2; **same-origin preconnect only**.
- **Policy locks.** Self-host by default; **no third-party JS on the public LCP/critical path** (analytics = first-party hashed deferred beacon). A genuinely-unavoidable external (e.g. a payment SDK, map provider, or bot-challenge) is allowed only if it is **CSP-allowlisted (named origin), deferred, and kept off the critical path** so it costs ~0 Lighthouse points ÔÇö never a wildcard, never blocking render. Add field RUM/CrUX monitoring post-launch (lab Ôëá field on low-end Jakarta 4G).

### 7.2 SEO & answer-engine optimization
- **SSR-first, JS-independent public surface.** Every public route serves **all** indexable content in raw first-byte HTML: name, NAP/postal address, geo, hours, price/`AggregateOffer`, `AggregateRating`, breadcrumbs, JSON-LD ÔÇö **zero dependency** on JS/Livewire/`wire:navigate`. Rationale: AI/answer crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot) execute **no JS**, and Googlebot's two-wave render is unreliable. `wire:navigate` is allowed only as a transition over already-complete SSR documents.
- **Server-rendered JSON-LD (validated in CI):** `SportsActivityLocation` (PostalAddress, geo, `openingHoursSpecification`, telephone, priceRange, image), `Offer`/`AggregateOffer` (IDR), `AggregateRating` + `Review` from `venue_reviews`, `BreadcrumbList`, `FAQPage` on venue pages, `Organization` + `WebSite` `SearchAction` on home. Exactly one self-referential `<link rel=canonical>`; consistent OG/Twitter; `<html lang="id">`.
- **SEO CI gate, separate from Lighthouse.** Lighthouse `seo ÔëÑ 0.99` is necessary but insufficient (ignores JSON-LD correctness / canonical / NAP). Add a **structured-data + meta validator per route**, plus a **JS-disabled fetch** asserting `<title>`, single self-canonical, meta description, exactly one `<h1>`, and parseable JSON-LD on every public route.
- **URL / crawl architecture.** `/{sport}/{city}` district pages are the primary local-landing pages (unique localized Bahasa copy, **gated by the liquidity threshold** so no thin pages publish). Chunked, accurate-`lastmod` **sitemap index** regenerated on venue create/update. `robots.txt`: allow verified search + AI crawlers on public GET; disallow `/dashboard`, `/admin`, booking-confirmation, and **all faceted filter-permutation query params** (crawl traps); indexable facets use crawlable `<a href>`, never JS-only.
- **Local SEO is two-sided.** Venue onboarding captures/links each venue's **Google Business Profile**; VenuePro acts as a **NAP-consistency engine** across site + GBP + IG/WA, rendered as SSR `<address>` + `PostalAddress` JSON-LD matching GBP exactly; admin surfaces a "claim/verify GBP" checklist.
- **Reviews as a ranking asset.** Render review count/score as crawlable SSR text + `AggregateRating`; post-play WA/email prompt (reuses the notification pipeline) grows review velocity. Render a short crawlable cancellation-policy summary on the public venue page.
- **i18n future-proofing.** Ship `<html lang="id">` in v1; design the URL/template layer for EN-later via `/en/` path prefix with reciprocal `hreflang` + `x-default` + per-locale self-canonical. Never geo-redirect or auto-translate. IDR/WIB in schema and visible text.
- **No cloaking.** Same SSR HTML to users and bots; reverse-DNS-verified crawler allowlist exempt from JS-challenge/rate-limits **on public GET only**; challenges apply solely to POST/auth/OTP. CI verifies production CSP does not strip `<script type="application/ld+json">`.

### 7.3 PWA & mobile-app readiness (fast native release, one codebase)
- **Two routing surfaces, one codebase.** Public = independent SSR multi-page documents (deep-linked *into*, never the installed-app home). Authenticated dashboard/admin behind `/dashboard` and `/admin` = persistent app-shell SPA (static top bar, bottom tab nav, content region, `wire:navigate`, zero full-page reloads in-shell). Native shells add only deltas behind capability detection (`window.Capacitor?.isNativePlatform()`, display-mode media query) ÔÇö **never a forked UI**.
- **Complete packaging-ready manifest in v1.** Serve `/manifest.webmanifest` (correct MIME, same origin) with `name`, `short_name`, `id`, `start_url '/dashboard?source=pwa'` (installed app opens to the operator tool, not marketing home), `scope '/'`, `display standalone`, `display_override [standalone,minimal-ui]`, `orientation portrait`, brand `theme_color`/`background_color`, `lang id`, `dir ltr`, `categories [sports,business]`, **self-hosted icons 192/512 + dedicated 512 maskable + monochrome badge**, `shortcuts` (Today's bookings, New walk-in, Credit top-up), narrow+wide screenshots. Validate installability in CI.
- **Android TWA readiness.** Serve `/.well-known/assetlinks.json` from a **Laravel route (never SW-cached)** with `delegate_permission/common.handle_all_urls`, package `asia.venuepro.app`, and an **array** of SHA-256 fingerprints (upload key + **Play App Signing** from the Play Console). Keep a checked-in Bubblewrap/`twa-manifest.json` config. The TWA points at the same live origin scoring Lighthouse 99 (real Chrome, not a thin wrapper). Plan for the **May-2026 Play closed-testing rule** (12 testers / 14 days) in the launch timeline.
- **iOS = bundled Capacitor shell + versioned API, NOT remote-origin WebView.** Do **not** point `Capacitor server.url` at the live site (production-discouraged; the classic App-Store **4.2 thin-wrapper rejection**). Bundle the app-shell web build locally; talk to Laravel via a **versioned Sanctum (Bearer) JSON API** for data/mutations; use **Capacitor OTA** (Capgo) for fast web-layer updates without re-review; set `limitsNavigationsToAppBoundDomains`. Add genuine native value to clear 4.2: APNs push, biometric unlock, native share/add-to-calendar, haptics, offline booking queue, secure storage.
- **Wrapper-safe auth & session.** Session/auth cookies `Secure; HttpOnly; SameSite=Lax` (**not Strict** ÔÇö Lax survives OTP/redirect/Xendit-return/deep-link handoffs through Custom Tabs & WKWebView) with a fixed domain `.venuepro.asia`. CSRF stays on all web/Livewire state-changing routes (browser/TWA); the iOS bundled app uses the Bearer API. **Acceptance gate:** auth round-trips successfully inside both a TWA Custom Tab and a Capacitor WKWebView (not a desktop-Chrome assumption).
- **Surface/method-aware self-healing service worker.** **Never** cache `/livewire/update`, `/dashboard/*`, `/admin/*`, any non-GET, any `Set-Cookie`/`Cache-Control: private` response, or booking/availability JSON (network-only). Precache + SWR **only** public SSR routes, hashed Vite assets, self-hosted fonts, static map facade. Public HTML = network-first passing through true 200/301/404/410 (never an app-shell fallback for public navs). Cache named by **build-id**; activate evicts stale; `skipWaiting`+`clients.claim` behind an in-app "update available" toast + kill-switch so a wrapped app never sticks on a stale shell.
- **Deep/universal links defined now.** Canonical https patterns (`/{sport}/{city}/{venue-slug}`, `/venue/{slug}`, `/venue/{slug}/book`, `/dashboard/bookings/{id}`, `/pay/{ref}`) round-trip identically in browser, TWA (`handle_all_urls` + `autoVerify` intent-filter), and iOS (Universal Links). Reserve `/.well-known/apple-app-site-association` now. Every deep-linkable URL renders a valid standalone page (cold deep-link never 404s); push payloads carry a `deep_link`.
- **Push as a swappable token source.** `push_subscriptions` (user_id, platform `[webpush|fcm|apns]`, endpoint/token, keys, ua, last_seen) + a provider-agnostic Notification channel so booking-confirmation/reminder/low-credit events fan out to push exactly as to WA/email. Web push now (VAPID, SW `push`+`notificationclick`ÔåÆ`clients.openWindow`, permission **primed after the first successful booking**, fired only from a user gesture); native later (Capacitor delivers FCM/APNs tokens into the same table ÔÇö no business-logic change).

### 7.4 Security & OWASP controls
- **Webhook is the only money mutator ÔÇö unforgeable, idempotent, replay-proof, tenant-bound.** Verify Xendit `x-callback-token` via `hash_equals` (constant-time). **Critical:** the token is a *static shared* token, not a per-message HMAC ÔÇö so additionally re-fetch/assert the payment by `provider_ref` **and** match the event's sub-account/`business_id` to the booking's `venue_id` (reject mismatch). `INSERT event_id` into `webhook_events` (UNIQUE) **before** side-effects. Enforce the allowed-transition matrix via conditional `UPDATE ... WHERE status='hold' AND hold_expires_at > now()` (0 rows = late/dup). Re-validate `amount_idr` + currency against the stored total (blocks amount-tampering). Route is CSRF-exempt but **IP-allowlisted to Xendit ranges**, no auth session, fast 200 + queued side-effects. Credit deduction and any venue refund happen **only** inside this verified idempotent path.
- **xenPlatform `for-user-id` confused-deputy control (top money-IDOR).** The platform master key can act on **any** sub-account via `for-user-id`. Centralize **all** Xendit calls in one gateway service that derives `for-user-id` strictly from the **route-bound authorized `venue_id`** (never request input), asserting the target sub-account belongs to the acting tenant before any charge/refund. Refunds are policy-authorized, **OTP/re-auth-gated**, idempotent (keyed by booking), with the refund % **server-computed** from the `CancellationPolicy` (player cannot influence) and their own audit row; alert on abnormal refund volume per venue.
- **Allowlist-based CSP** via shared middleware (pragmatic, not absolutist). The **one hard line** stays: `script-src` has **no `unsafe-inline`, no `unsafe-eval`** (`'self' 'nonce-{n}' 'strict-dynamic'`; Livewire 3 CSP-safe build + `@alpinejs/csp`; public pages use a **static hash-based** `script-src`, cacheable). Beyond that, CSP is an **allowlist**: self-host by default, but where an asset/service genuinely cannot be self-hosted, add its **specific origin** to the relevant directive ÔÇö **named host, never wildcard `*`**, each entry recorded with a reason in a central `config/csp.php` allowlist (single source of truth, reviewed in PRs). `style-src 'self' 'nonce-{n}'` on the inline critical-CSS block; components toggle token **classes**, never inline `:style`. Base directives: `default-src 'self'`; `img-src 'self' data:`; `base-uri`/`form-action 'self'`; `frame-ancestors 'none'`; `object-src 'none'`; `upgrade-insecure-requests`; Trusted Types where supported.
- **Documented external allowlist (the only permitted non-`self` origins; off the public critical path).** `connect-src`/`script-src`: **Xendit** (`https://*.xendit.co` checkout/SDK + return), **Capacitor** (`capacitor://localhost`) + the **TWA host**; **bot-challenge** (Cloudflare Turnstile / hCaptcha) only on auth/OTP/booking POST endpoints (noindex, off the LCP path); **map tiles** are self-hosted PMTiles by default, but if a hosted tile/style provider is ever needed it is added here as a named `img-src`/`connect-src` origin behind the click-to-load facade. **WhatsApp/FCM/APNs/VAPID** are server-side (no browser origin). Anything not on this list is blocked; adding to it requires the documented reason + "can't be self-hosted" justification + a Lighthouse/critical-path check.
- **OTP / WhatsApp abuse ÔÇö SMS-pumping, brute-force, enumeration.** Composite-key limiters ÔÇö `otp-send` `perMinute(1)->by(phone)` AND daily cap (~5/day/phone) AND per-IP (~10/hr) AND per-subnet ÔÇö with exponential cooldowns; a **channel-cost circuit breaker** (hard daily WA send budget + spend alerts, auto-throttle on spikes). OTPs 6-digit, **HMAC-hashed**, single-use, 5-min TTL, max 5 verify attempts then invalidate (Redis keyed by E.164-normalized phone so `08ÔÇª`/`+62ÔÇª` can't bypass). **Constant-time, identical responses** for existing/non-existing accounts. CAPTCHA/Turnstile after threshold, on the POST endpoint only ÔÇö never on the SSR/LCP path. Re-issue session ID on login; OTP re-challenge for refund execution, credit top-up, phone change.
- **Multi-tenancy / IDOR.** Bind active venue from the **route** (never `venue_id` from request body); Policies on bookings/payments/credits/refunds/reviews/settings checking `$user->venue_id === $model->venue_id`; `Gate::before` grants platform_admin **only**; resolve children via parent (`$venue->bookings()->findOrFail($id)`). In Livewire mark ownership props `#[Locked]` and re-authorize inside every action. **Blocking Pest isolation suite:** staff of Venue A get 403/404 on every Venue B resource and money action.
- **Mass-assignment, validation, raw-PostGIS SQLi.** Explicit FormRequests/DTOs, never `request()->all()`; guard `role, venue_id, status, balance, xendit_subaccount_id, price_idr, service_fee_idr, rating_avg` (set server-side). PostGIS `ST_DWithin`/geography + full-text always use **bound parameters** (never interpolate lat/lng/radius/search); clamp radius; allowlist sport/city/slug. Server **recomputes** price + service fee + credit cost authoritatively at hold/confirm.
- **File upload, SSRF, headers.** Venue images ÔÇö `finfo` magic-byte + extension allowlist (jpg/png/webp), size cap, re-encode/strip EXIF (GPS/PII), store off web-root with random names, serve via signed URLs, no SVG unless sanitized. SSRF on any server-side fetch (website/IG/WA/image URL): https-only, host allowlist, block RFC1918/link-local/169.254.169.254, no internal redirects. Xendit return URLs **server-constructed** from booking id (never reflected from query). Headers: HSTS preload, `X-Content-Type-Options nosniff`, `Referrer-Policy strict-origin-when-cross-origin`, minimal `Permissions-Policy`, force HTTPS. CORS first-party only.
- **PII / UU PDP (Law 27/2022, fines up to 2% revenue).** Documented lawful basis per purpose (contract for booking; **separate, granular, withdrawable consent** for WA marketing/push, logged with timestamp). **Encrypt phone numbers + booking history at rest** (encrypted casts + blind-index/HMAC for lookup); **mask phone** in UI/logs (last 3ÔÇô4 digits). Data-subject rights flows (access/export, rectification, erasure, consent withdrawal) with SLAs; **72-hour breach runbook**. Treat Xendit + WA BSP as **processors** (DPAs). Scrub PII from error tracking/logs. **Device push tokens are personal data** ÔÇö in retention/export/erasure scope.
- **Published Privacy Policy + Terms pages (public SSR, indexable).** Plain-Bahasa policy stating data collected (phone, booking history, device/push tokens), purposes + lawful basis, processors (Xendit, WA BSP), retention, data-subject rights, and how to delete the account; versioned, with a stable canonical URL referenced from the manifest, app-store listings, signup consent, and footer. The booking/marketing consent records link the **policy version** in effect.
- **Account deletion ("Hapus akun") ÔÇö self-service, in-app + web.** A clear settings action that: (1) requires **OTP/re-auth**; (2) shows what happens (future bookings cancelled, WA/marketing consent withdrawn, push tokens revoked, personal data erased); (3) blocks/handles deletion with **active holds or unsettled payments** gracefully; (4) **anonymizes rather than hard-deletes** records tied to financial/audit obligations (bookings/payments keep amounts + timestamps but strip name/phone, replaced by a tombstone user) so venue accounting and the audit trail stay intact; (5) is **idempotent + audit-logged** (request, confirmation, completion). For **venue_owner**, deletion is gated on settling/transferring the venue (can't orphan a live venue with future bookings). A grace-period soft-delete (e.g. 14 days, account disabled + recoverable) precedes the purge job; provide a parallel **email/WA request channel** for users who can't sign in.
- **Consent/disclosure server-enforced.** The refund/fee acknowledgement is an affirmative, unchecked-by-default, **server-gated, versioned, audit-logged** consent event (policy version + timestamp + user) ÔÇö booking cannot transition to a payable charge without it.
- **Business-logic abuse + audit/secrets/supply-chain.** Rate-limit hold creation per user/IP, cap concurrent holds, require a verified (OTP) session before a paid hold, robust expired-hold release (queue+retry, cancels the pending Xendit charge), credit-deduct + refund-reversal **atomic and idempotent** (exactly once, never negative/double-spent), "insufficient credits ÔåÆ bookings paused" fails safe. **Append-only, externally-shipped audit trail** for money + security events (OTP send/verify, login/failure, role change, policy edits, credit topup/deduct/reversal, refund executed, consent given, webhook receipt+decision with `event_id`) ÔÇö actor/tenant/IP/UA/timestamp + before/after, PII masked. **Secrets vaulted + rotated** (Xendit master+sub keys, `x-callback-token`, WA BSP, DB/Redis, `APP_KEY`, VAPID), never in repo/manifest/client; Redis password-protected. `composer audit` + `npm audit`/Dependabot + Enlightn in CI; `APP_DEBUG=false`; Telescope/Horizon behind auth+IP allowlist.

### 7.5 UX & usability principles ("mudah digunakan & nyaman")
- **Booking-flow ergonomics + instant feedback.** discover ÔåÆ slot ÔåÆ pay completes in minimal taps with feedback **within 100 ms of every tap**. Rule: **user feedback is always client-instant** (slot press, selected/disabled state, hold countdown = pure Alpine + CSS transform/opacity, ~0 ms TBT), **truth is always server-authoritative** (debounced Livewire, optimistic UI first). On a `23P01` "slot just taken" conflict, show a calm inline message + auto-refresh the grid ÔÇö never a raw error or dead end.
- **Touch, tap-target & safe-area.** Every interactive element **ÔëÑ 44├ù44 pt** hit area (invisible hit-slop where the visible chip is compact) with ÔëÑ 8 px spacing; `touch-action: manipulation`. All fixed chrome padded with `env(safe-area-inset-*)` for notch/Dynamic-Island/gesture bar in browser, TWA, Capacitor. Bottom nav capped at **5** items (Calendar, Bookings, Credits, Reviews, More), icon+label.
- **Accessibility WCAG 2.2 AA as a Lighthouse-99 co-requirement.** `focus-visible` ring on every interactive element; semantic landmarks + sequential headings; `aria-label` on icon-only buttons; **booking-state and all functional color carry an icon/text label** (never color alone); form errors via `role=alert`/`aria-live`; `prefers-reduced-motion` respected; text scaling without truncation.
- **Form & input UX (the OTP/phone + payment choke point).** Visible labels (not placeholder-only); `inputmode`/`type=tel` for phone, numeric + `autocomplete=one-time-code` for OTP (autofills from SMS/WA); **ÔëÑ 16 px font on all inputs** (no iOS/WKWebView zoom); inline validation on blur; errors below the field with cause+fix; auto-focus first invalid field; loading+disabled on every async submit. OTP error copy is single, friendly, **non-revealing yet actionable** with a visible resend cooldown ("Jika nomor terdaftar, kode telah dikirim ┬À Kirim ulang 0:30"); lockout shows "coba lagi dalam X menit" + WhatsApp-support fallback, never a dead end.
- **Refund/cancellation disclosure as progressive disclosure.** Compact always-visible policy summary chip next to the price (**calm caution tokens, not alarm-red**), tap-to-expand full venue policy, and a **required unchecked acknowledgement** ("Saya mengerti kebijakan venue") immediately above the pay button ÔÇö server-enforced + audit-logged. Prominence via **placement + required interaction**, not visual aggression.
- **State preservation, deep-linking & back integrity.** Filter/sort/date/tab state lives in the **URL query string** on public discovery AND the dashboard, so `wire:navigate` back, refresh-inside-wrapper, share, and deep links restore scroll+state losslessly. Multi-field dashboard forms auto-save drafts so an accidental wrapper dismissal never loses work.
- **Trust, loading, empty & offline states.** Skeleton/shimmer (not blank spinners) for any wait > 300 ms; clear booking-confirmation success state ("WA confirmation incoming"); helpful empty states with a next action; visible "tersedia per HH:MM" timestamp on SWR availability; calm offline fallback; confirmation dialog before destructive actions (cancel booking, issue refund) with undo where feasible.
- **Launch-blocking UX gaps owned in v1:** (1) **cold-start / empty inventory** ÔÇö a 0ÔÇô2-venue radius result shows "nearest cluster + WhatsApp the venue", never a blank page; (2) **walk-in / staff entry** ÔÇö a one-screen, few-tap walk-in booking through the same EXCLUDE constraint (must beat pen-and-paper or adoption fails); (3) **notifications inbox** + permission-priming after the first successful booking (also clears Apple 4.2); (4) **hold-timer expiry** ÔÇö visible calm countdown, T-2min nudge, graceful "hold expired, slot offered again if still free"; (5) **i18n copy as a reviewed string catalogue** (Bahasa primary), never improvised in templates.

2. **Service worker vs auth/tenant security vs SEO freshness vs frozen wrapper.** Surface/method-aware self-healing SW: never cache `/livewire/update`, `/dashboard|/admin`, non-GET, `Set-Cookie`/`private`, or availability JSON; public HTML network-first passing true status codes; hashed static cache-first; build-id cache versioning + update toast/kill-switch.
3. **Strict CSP vs inline critical CSS + Alpine/Livewire + caching/TTFB.** Split by directive/surface: `script-src` strict everywhere (Livewire CSP-safe + `@alpinejs/csp`, one hashed external JS, no inline); public CSP static hash-based (cacheable), nonce only on the dynamic dashboard; components toggle classes not `:style`.
4. **iOS thin web-wrapper vs Apple 4.2 + XSS blast radius.** iOS = bundled Capacitor shell + versioned Sanctum Bearer API + OTA + genuine native value; Android TWA legitimately same-origin. One codebase, packaged two ways.
